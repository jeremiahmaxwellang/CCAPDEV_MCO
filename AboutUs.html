About Labyrinth

We built Labyrinth to provide students with an easy way to reserve lab slots, check availability, and streamline on-campus resourcesâ€”all in one place.

Our mission is to enhance student productivity through an intuitive and seamless scheduling experience. Labyrinth offers an efficient, student-friendly platform built on modern UI principles. We believe in accessible, easy-to-use tools that empower students to manage their academic schedules with ease.

Labyrinth was developed by a team of student developers from DLSU as part of the requirements for the CCAPDEV course under Sir Arturo Caronongan. The team is composed of developers, designers, and thinkers dedicated to making academic life smoother through smart digital solutions.
